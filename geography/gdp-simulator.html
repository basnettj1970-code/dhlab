Edexcel A Level Economics — National Income
GDP = C + I + G + (X−M). Consumption is typically the largest component (~60% in the UK). The Keynesian multiplier shows how an injection of spending generates a larger rise in GDP. Key debates: fiscal vs monetary policy; austerity vs stimulus; trade deficit management.
