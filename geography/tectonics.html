Plate boundary types
⬅️➡️
Constructive
Plates move apart. Magma rises to fill gap. Mid-ocean ridges, rift valleys, shield volcanoes.
➡️⬅️
Destructive
Denser oceanic plate subducts. Deep ocean trenches, explosive volcanoes, strong earthquakes.
⬆️⬇️
Collision
Two continental plates collide. Mountain ranges, fold mountains. No volcanoes but severe earthquakes.
↔️
Conservative
Plates slide past each other. No volcanoes. Frequent, shallow, devastating earthquakes.
