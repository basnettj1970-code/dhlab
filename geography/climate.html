Key climate change impacts (OCR H481 focus)

🌊
Sea level rise
Global mean sea level has risen ~20cm since 1900, accelerating since 1990. Threatens coastal settlements, small island states (Maldives, Tuvalu), and estuarine ecosystems. UK: Thames Barrier increasingly used.
🔥
Extreme weather events
Frequency and intensity of heatwaves, wildfires, and flooding is increasing. 2022 Pakistan floods (33m displaced), 2021 BC wildfires, 2019-20 Australian 'Black Summer' — all linked to climate change.
🧊
Arctic & glacial melting
Arctic sea ice extent declining ~13% per decade. Greenland ice sheet melting accelerating. Himalayan glaciers (water source for 2bn people) retreating rapidly. Albedo feedback loop amplifies warming.
🌿
Ecosystem disruption
Coral bleaching (50% of Great Barrier Reef affected since 2016), shifting biome boundaries, species range changes. Ocean acidification (pH fallen 0.1 since industrialisation) threatens marine ecosystems.
🌾
Food & water security
Changing precipitation patterns affect agricultural yields. Sahel desertification, reduced Himalayan meltwater, and shifting monsoons threaten billions. Climate change estimated to reduce yields 10% per decade of warming.
🏃
Climate migration
By 2050, 216 million people may be forced to move within their countries due to climate impacts. Low-lying Bangladesh, Sahel Africa, and Pacific islands face the greatest displacement risk — linking to Paper 2 migration.
